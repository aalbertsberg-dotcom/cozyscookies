Our Story

Two Guys,
Two Dogs,
One Kitchen.

Cozy's Cookies started the same way most great things do — because we couldn't find what we were looking for. Store-bought treats are full of junk, and our dogs deserved better.

So we started baking. What began as a Sunday experiment turned into something the whole neighborhood wanted in. We're just two guys who love their dogs and believe simple ingredients make the best treats.

Everything is baked in small batches, locally sourced where we can, and made with the same love we'd want for our own pups.
